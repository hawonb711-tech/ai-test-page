Global Cloud Computing Market Analysis — Q1 2026

The global cloud computing market reached $720 billion in Q1 2026, representing a 22% year-over-year growth rate. Amazon Web Services (AWS) continues to lead with 31% market share, followed by Microsoft Azure at 25% and Google Cloud at 11%.

Key Trends

Artificial intelligence workloads now account for 40% of cloud spending, up from 15% in 2024. Companies are increasingly adopting multi-cloud strategies to avoid vendor lock-in.

Regional Analysis

North America remains the largest market, but Asia-Pacific is growing fastest at 35% annually. Europe's growth is moderated by regulatory compliance costs.
