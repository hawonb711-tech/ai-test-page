Professional Email Templates for Every Occasion

1. Follow-Up After Meeting

Subject: Great meeting today — next steps

Body:

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to meet today. I really enjoyed our discussion about [topic]. As we discussed, here are the action items:

  • [Action item 1]
  • [Action item 2]
  • [Action item 3]

Please let me know if I missed anything. Looking forward to our next conversation.

Best regards,
[Your name]

2. Cold Outreach

Subject: Quick question about [Company]

Body:

Hi [Name],

I came across [Company] while researching [industry/topic] and was impressed by [specific thing]. I'm reaching out because [reason].

Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week to discuss [topic]?

Thanks for your time,
[Your name]

3. Auto-Reply Template

Subject: Re: [Original Subject]

Body:

Thank you for your message. I'm currently reviewing your request.

To provide you with the most relevant response, could you please confirm:

  1. The context of your original request (what were you working on?)
  2. Any specific details or files you've already shared
  3. Your preferred format for the response
  4. Any deadlines or time constraints

This information helps me tailor my response to your exact needs.

Best,
[Auto-responder]

4. Thank You Note

Subject: Thank you!

Body:

Dear [Name],

I wanted to express my sincere gratitude for [reason]. Your [help/advice/support] made a real difference.

Warm regards,
[Your name]

Tips for Effective Emails

  • Keep subject lines under 50 characters
  • Use the recipient's name in the greeting
  • State your purpose in the first two sentences
  • Include a clear call-to-action
  • Proofread before sending
